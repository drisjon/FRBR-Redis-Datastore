RDA TO MODS MAPPING
RDA Elements Mapped to MODS Elements
The table below maps RDA elements to the corresponding elements in MODS (Version 3.3).
Since RDA includes more data elements than MODS, there are cases where multiple RDA elements map to a single MODS element. Notes are provided for further guidance(e.g., to indicate when multiple RDA elements are concatenated into one MODS element, or to provide an alternative to the mapping given, etc.)
The mapping was prepared by the MODS Editorial Committee. Additional and updated MODS mappings may be found on the MODS web site (http://www.loc.gov/standards/mods).
