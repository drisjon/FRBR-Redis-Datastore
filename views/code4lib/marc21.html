MARC21

MARC21 is the current data carrier for library bibliographic records.

  • Legacy and wide-spread
  • Interoperability is a core requirement of LOC Bibliographic Framework
  • Colorado College's currently uses its legacy ILS to manage over 860,000 MARC21 records for print holdings and purchased electronic resources
