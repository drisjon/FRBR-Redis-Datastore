What about Relational Databases?

A relational database, from Oracle, Microsoft, MySQL, or Postgre, and used by many current ILS systems to store MARC records offer one alternative but lack the flexibility to represent arbitrarily data schemas and have issues scaling out to hundreds of millions of records

What about Solr?

Solr addresses a different problem than data storage and is more of search and retrieval technology than a NoSQL datastore. Although there is often overlap when a full-text searching is implemented in an SQL database index or NoSQL index. While currently in planning, ${project.name} will use Solr for end-user and application searching of FRBR entities and retrieve entities.
