The Galatic Catalog

Charles Stross Laundry Series

Magic is just computational realisim, making what we do even more voodoo. Do you ever imagine being a cataloger in a Lovercraftain, Lucas, Poter, Dungeons and Dragons, or Tokken universies?

However! Unlike all of those other fields, we as librarians, information technologists, and curious individuals can and do apply imaginary topics to never existing artifacts using the same technology we use for tangible objects.

So imagine your FRBR artifacts cataloged with your communitiy's metadata branes, creating the augmented world of smart splimes.
