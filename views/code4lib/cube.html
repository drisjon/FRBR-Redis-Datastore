Bibliographic Cube

The underlying concept of the FRBR Redis Datastore is the Biblographic Cube. Instead of trying to creating a single, flat canonical biblographic record; the datastore orchastractes a network of key-value biblographic associations with FRBR entities.

This is better visualized as moving from a flat record (Dublin Core, MARC21, MODS XML, etc.) to a cube of FRBR entities interseting with parallel planes of metadata.
