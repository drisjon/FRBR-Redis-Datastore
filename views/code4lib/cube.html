Bibliographic Cube

One way to visualize the underlying concepts behind ${project.name} is to think of a information organization around a Bibliographic Cube. Instead of trying to creating a single, flat canonical bibliographic record; the datastore orchestrates a network of key-value bibliographic associations with FRBR entities that are stacked upon or next to each other, making up a cube>

This is better visualized as moving from a flat record (Dublin Core, MARC21, MODS XML, etc.) to a cube of FRBR entities intersecting with parallel planes of metadata called branes.
