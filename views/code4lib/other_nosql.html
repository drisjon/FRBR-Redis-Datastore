Other NoSQL

There are many other NoSQL datastore technologies including document stores like CouchDB and MongoDB. More well-known graph datastores FlockDB and Neo4j.

Facebook donated their Cassandra technology to the Apache foundation, that along with Amazon's Dynamo are examples of eventually‐consistent key‐value stores.
