## START Colorado College MARC21 & DACC MODS DC Records DIV

CC's MARC21 & DACC

Full import of Colorado College's 875,590 MARC21 records and 3,500 MODS records from Digital Archives into FRBR Redis Native datastore.

Next, in collaboration with the Colorado Alliance of Research Libraries, scale and import the Alliance collection of MARC and ADR records at a Consortuium scale to see how well Redis handles workflows at that increased scale and usage frequency. Redis publish/subscribe holds real promise for optimizing realtime lending of FRBR items in a successfully inter library-loan service in the inter-mountain West.

Statistically mapping of metadata

After we reach a certain critical mass of ingested material, we'll be able to look at the Redis statistical distribution of attributes. These attibutes can be analized and optimized as Redis data primiatives and offer interesting research possibilities of automatic metadata recognition.

Another statistical relation is being able to directly map usage analytics to FRBR bibliographic attributes as they are used by our users in realtime. ## END DIV
