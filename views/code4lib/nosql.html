Why Redis?

The key-value Redis.io cache nature means Redis is fast, small, and easy is to deploy, key requirements of the LOC bibliographic frameworks and cructial for smaller and leaner libraries and cultural information technology organizations.

  • Fast, C code
  • Multiple language bindings including Python, Ruby, PHP, Perl, C#, and Java.
  • Easy to represent multi-deminsional nature of bibliographic records
